ABOUT ME

I'm a self-taught digital artist and game design student with additional experience in writing and coding.

I truly believe that games are the future of interactive arts and the visual language of a game affects both what a game means to a player and how it feels to interact with such a game. I love both playing and analyzing games of all kinds. I am often drawn to games that place a heavy emphasis on story and strategic thinking in turn-based combat though I also enjoy action games that test a player's skill and willpower.

I tend to enjoy illustrating and depicting scenes of our colorful daily lives. The graphic design seen in magazines also inspires me in how I compose my art. I aim to create dynamic pieces that tell a story while being interesting to look at. I also do work in graphic design and animation.
